so pieces to a dungeon:
    stairs
    doors
    passages
    chambers
    exits
    connections

How they interact:
    doors either lead nowhere or to a chamber or passages
    passages lead to dead ends, other passages, chambers, or doors, and often more than one
    chambers lead to passages, or doors, also often more than one.

What properties does a chamber have:
    width and height
    number of connectors
    attached to walls

what do passages have:
    width and a relative height, can and often contains turns and splits
    number of connectors

what do doors have:
    doors are always at a connection point, sometimes may end connection with false door
